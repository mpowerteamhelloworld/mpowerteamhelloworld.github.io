HELLO WORLD

# Where everything begins....

Hello World is an 8 membered team formed during the mPower training workshop on Power Communication conducted for second year BTech Computer Science and Engineering students from 01 March 2018 to 03 March 2018. During these days we will be engaged with alot of activities that will help us develop our soft skills. Points are given for every activity conducted and a rank list is created among the 8 teams formed at the start of the program. On the final day, the team with the most points will be declared as the mPower champions with certificates and medals for each member. At the end of first day we stand at second position with 329 points. We look forward to getting the championship award and learn alot during the process.
